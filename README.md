# Global AI, ML & Data Science Salaries - Exploratory Data Analysis

## Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Global AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science Salaries dataset. The objective is to analyze salary trends and understand how factors such as experience level, employment type, company size, work location, and remote work influence employee salaries. The project provides meaningful business insights through statistical analysis and data visualization.

---

## Problem Statement

Salary structures in the AI industry vary significantly across countries, organizations, and job roles. Without proper analysis, organizations may find it difficult to design competitive salary packages or understand workforce trends.

This project analyzes global salary data to identify the key factors affecting employee compensation and support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Business Objective

The primary objectives of this project are to:

- Perform complete Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
- Analyze salary distribution across different employee groups.
- Study the impact of experience level, employment type, and company size on salaries.
- Explore remote work trends in the AI industry.
- Generate business insights to support recruitment and salary planning.

## Key Insights

- Experience level has a significant impact on salary.
- Senior and Executive professionals earn the highest salaries.
- Full-Time employment is the most common employment type.
- Medium and Large companies generally offer better salary packages.
- Remote work is widely adopted in AI-related jobs.
- AI job opportunities are concentrated in major technology-driven countries.
